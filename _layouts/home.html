Introduction

We are thrilled to convey the exciting news that the esteemed "International Conference on Extreme Value Theory and Quantitative Risk Management" (EVT & QRM) is scheduled to be held at Fudan University, located in the vibrant city of Shanghai, China, from July 29th to July 31st, 2024. This conference promises to be a unique gathering of experts, researchers, and practitioners from various fields who share a keen interest in the evolving landscape of extreme value theory and quantitative risk management.

In today's increasingly complex and uncertain world, extreme value theory and quantitative risk management have become indispensable tools for understanding, assessing, and mitigating risks across a wide range of industries. From finance to insurance, from network science to healthcare, the ability to analyze extreme events and quantify their potential impacts is crucial for making informed decisions and protecting against potential losses.
